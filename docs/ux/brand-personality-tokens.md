# Brand Personality Tokens

Use this file to translate abstract brand adjectives into practical design
decisions.

These are not implementation tokens like `--color-primary-500`. They are
directional tokens that help the team choose type, spacing, surface, motion,
and imagery with more consistency.

## How To Use

- Pick 2 to 4 primary personality tokens for a product.
- Record them in the visual direction brief.
- Use them to resolve design debates when multiple acceptable options exist.

## Starter Token Set

### Calm Utility

- Feels: stable, helpful, low-friction
- Typography: clear and restrained
- Spacing: generous and orderly
- Motion: subtle and confidence-building
- Avoid: flashy accents and hyperactive UI

### Editorial Precision

- Feels: curated, articulate, high-trust
- Typography: strong hierarchy and expressive headings
- Spacing: intentional whitespace and clean alignment
- Motion: minimal but refined
- Avoid: noisy dashboards and generic card grids

### Technical Sharpness

- Feels: exact, powerful, high-signal
- Typography: crisp and structured
- Spacing: tight but readable
- Motion: fast and functional
- Avoid: soft, vague, or overly playful treatment

### Premium Restraint

- Feels: elevated, composed, expensive
- Typography: elegant contrast with disciplined scale
- Spacing: spacious with few accents
- Motion: smooth and measured
- Avoid: clutter, novelty effects, and loud color use

### Playful Momentum

- Feels: energetic, optimistic, inviting
- Typography: lively and characterful
- Spacing: dynamic but controlled
- Motion: expressive with clear cause and effect
- Avoid: childish noise and loss of task clarity

### Warm Guidance

- Feels: humane, supportive, reassuring
- Typography: approachable and readable
- Spacing: comfortable and forgiving
- Motion: gentle feedback and orientation
- Avoid: harsh contrast patterns that feel cold or punitive

## Roost Mapping

### Chosen Personality Tokens

- Primary: Technical Sharpness, Premium Restraint.
- Secondary: Calm Utility.

### Translation Notes

- Typography: Inter by default, crisp weights, compact hierarchy, micro labels
  in uppercase with measured tracking.
- Color: dark operating-center base with indigo, blue, and cyan digital light;
  green only for healthy state.
- Surfaces: modular dark glass, thin outlines, subtle depth, restrained glow.
- Motion: smooth fades, focus glow, sync/data-flow cues, and calm transitions.
- Imagery / illustration: outline geometry, nest/orbit/module connection
  motifs, and future logo exploration around sparrow, nest, `R`, orbit, and
  system nodes.
- Interaction tone: confident, practical, and command-oriented; no playful
  bounce, no cyberpunk noise, no fake status theater.
